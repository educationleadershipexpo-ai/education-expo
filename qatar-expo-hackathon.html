The Challenge: AI in Personalized Learning

This year's theme focuses on leveraging Artificial Intelligence to create adaptive and personalized learning experiences for students. Your challenge is to develop a prototype that addresses one of the following tracks:

  • AI-powered tools for students with learning differences.
  • Adaptive learning platforms for STEM subjects.
  • Gamified language learning apps using generative AI.
  • AI mentors for career guidance and skill development.
