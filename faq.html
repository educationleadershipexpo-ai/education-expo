General Questions

Qatar Education Expo 2026 is the premier education event in the MENA region, connecting universities, EdTech companies, and education consultants with thousands of students, parents, and industry leaders. It's a two-day event focused on networking, recruitment, and thought leadership.

The expo will take place on April 19–20, 2026, at the Sheraton Grand Doha Resort & Convention Hotel in Qatar.

The event is ideal for Grade 11-12 students and their parents, university representatives, EdTech companies, education consultants, K-12 school leaders, government officials, and anyone involved in the education sector.

The Expo features university exhibitions, leadership talks, innovation workshops, and the lovable x Qatar Education Expo 2026 student hackathon. See the full Agenda for more details.

No, entry is completely free for all registered attendees (students, parents, educators, etc.). Only exhibitors and sponsors require a paid booking.

Standard booth packages typically include the physical booth space, walls, basic lighting, a table, and chairs. Higher-tier packages include more features like premium locations and furniture. For a full breakdown, please request our sponsorship deck via the Contact page.

Yes, speaking slots for workshops and panel discussions are available, primarily included in our Gold and Platinum sponsorship packages. If you are interested in speaking, please fill out the form on our Become a Speaker page or mention it in your inquiry on the Contact page.

Universities from outside Qatar are welcome to participate. You can exhibit through our standard or premium booth packages, or join as a sponsor for greater visibility. Our team can provide assistance with logistics and setup to ensure a smooth experience. Please contact us for more details.
